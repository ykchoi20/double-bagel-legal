1. Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

Welcome to Double Bagel. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information of our tennis captains and players. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application and services.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name and email address.
  • Tennis Profile Data: Skill level, play preferences, team affiliation, and availability for matches.
  • Captain-Specific Data: Roster names and match schedules created within the app.
  • Payment Information: All subscriptions are processed via the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not store your full credit card numbers on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to facilitate match scheduling, process your $1/month subscription, and provide customer support. We do not use GPS location services to track your movements.

Sharing of Information

Your name and availability are visible to your Captain and fellow team members to coordinate matches. We use trusted third-party service providers for cloud hosting and payment processing.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information only as long as your account is active.

  • How to Delete Your Data: You may initiate a permanent account deletion request directly within the app settings under "Delete Account" or by emailing support at double.bagel.2026@gmail.com.
  • What Happens Upon Deletion: Once a deletion request is processed, your personal profile, email, and specific availability data will be scrubbed from our active databases within 30 days.
  • Note on Team Data: Historical match scores or roster entries created by a Captain that do not contain your specific personal contact info may remain to maintain the integrity of league standings.
