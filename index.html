Double Bagel Legal Documents

1. Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

Welcome to Double Bagel. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information of our tennis captains and players. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application and services.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name and email address.
  • Tennis Profile Data: Skill level, play preferences, team affiliation, and availability for matches.
  • Captain-Specific Data: Roster names and match schedules created within the app.
  • Payment Information: All subscriptions are processed via the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not store your full credit card numbers on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to facilitate match scheduling, process your $1/month subscription, and provide customer support. We do not use GPS location services to track your movements.

Sharing of Information

Your name and availability are visible to your Captain and fellow team members to coordinate matches. We use trusted third-party service providers for cloud hosting and payment processing.

Data Retention and Deletion

We respect your right to control your personal data. When you choose to delete your account, we process the data as follows:

1. Permanently Deleted Information

The following data is immediately and irreversibly scrubbed from our active databases:

  • Identity & Auth: All login credentials (including Google Auth) and your core profile (Name, Email, Gender, Skill Level, Venmo handle, Playing Preferences, and USTA number).
  • Team & Play: All team memberships, availability responses for matches, lineup spots, and assigned tasks.
  • Social & Activity: Post reactions, comments, comment likes, post views, and any reported post records you filed.
  • Private Data: Venmo payment records, hidden team preferences, and any contact messages sent to support.

2. Anonymized Functional Records

To maintain the integrity of team schedules and league history for other users, certain "functional" records remain but are fully anonymized (all personal identifiers are wiped):

  • Lineups & Tasks: Records of cancelled lineups and tasks you created remain visible to Captains, but the "created by" or "cancelled by" fields are cleared.
  • Team Content: Posts you wrote remain visible on the team feed, but the author attribution is removed.
  • Team Management: Teams you created will continue to exist for their members, and records of player removals will persist, but your name will be removed from all "created by" or "removed by" attributions.

Note: Once you tap "Delete Account," this process happens immediately and cannot be undone.
